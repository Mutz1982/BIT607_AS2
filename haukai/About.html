About the Organisation

Haukai Restaurant specialises in Maori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands.
It was started by a couple in 2010 who were enthusiastic about creating a dinner menu that featured Maori and Pakeha kai options and drinks.
This coupled with a unique experience in te ao Maori by using karakia to bless the food and other Maori culture elements provides for a wonderful experience of Aotearoa.
